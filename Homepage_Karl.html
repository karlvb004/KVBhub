ABOUT US

This year, our class of 30 dedicated students has come together to present ECHO, a digital arts exhibition that showcases the culmination of months of hard work, creativity, and passion. Each of us has specialized in a unique area of art, ranging from the timeless beauty of traditional painting to the dynamic worlds of graphic novels, cutting-edge graphic design, and the compelling power of photography. Others among us have explored the immersive potential of animation and installation design, pushing the boundaries of storytelling and interaction. Despite our diverse artistic disciplines, we’re united by a shared love for creativity and a desire to communicate through our work, inviting our audience to see the world through our eyes.

Our exhibition isn’t just a showcase of technical skill or conceptual depth; it’s a celebration of the artistic journeys we’ve embarked on throughout our dissertations. We’ve poured countless hours into crafting these pieces, driven by our passion for storytelling, self-expression, and innovation. Together, we’ve curated a space that reflects not only our individual talents but also the collaborative spirit that has shaped this experience. Through ECHO, we hope to inspire others, spark conversations, and leave a lasting impression of what digital art can achieve when fueled by imagination and purpose.
